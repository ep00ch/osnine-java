     This  is the source package for the famous Crowther and Woods
adventure  game.   It  compiles with MicroWare  C.   This  source
compiles  to the object currently distributed, and, as such, does
run.  To any who are brave enough to enter here, good luck.
   MAKING CHANGES
1. Make your changes
2. compile any modified modules with the -r option
3. Run linkadv (after editing for your library drive).
    These files will be linked to make advent
       advent.r
       turn.r
       database.r
       english.r
       verb.r
       itverb.r
  Remember - advent needs at least 2 extra pages of memory!
